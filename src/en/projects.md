<div class="page-intro">
  <p class="eyebrow">Projects</p>
  <h1>Research Projects</h1>
  <p>His project work spans energy transition, climate discourse, inter-Korean environmental cooperation, and institutional research on environmental education.</p>
</div>

<div class="proj-item">
  <div class="proj-title">Study on Formulating a Regionally Collaborative Private RE100 Implementation Plan</div>
  <div class="proj-meta">Sept. 2025 ~ Jan. 2026 | Energy Transition Forum Korea</div>
  <div class="proj-desc">A policy study exploring how the private sector can implement RE100 (100% renewable electricity) through region-based collaboration.</div>
</div>

<div class="proj-item">
  <div class="proj-title">A Study on the Change of Climate Experts' Concerns and Points on Climate Change through Analyzing the Assessment Reports of the IPCC</div>
  <div class="proj-meta">Jun. 2023 ~ Dec. 2023 | National Research Foundation of Korea (Ministry of Education)</div>
  <div class="proj-desc">Tracked how climate experts' concerns and perceptions of climate change shifted over time by analyzing successive IPCC assessment reports.</div>
</div>

<div class="proj-item">
  <div class="proj-title">Exploring the Possibility of Inter-Korean Climate Change and Renewable Energy Research Cooperation through the Analysis of Domestic and International Research Trends of North Korean Researchers</div>
  <div class="proj-meta">Apr. 2023 ~ Jan. 2024 | The Institute for Peace and Unification Studies, Seoul National University</div>
  <div class="proj-desc">Analyzed North Korean climate and renewable-energy research trends to explore avenues for inter-Korean research cooperation.</div>
</div>

<div class="proj-item">
  <div class="proj-title">Publication of the White Paper Commemorating the 55th Anniversary of Environmental Graduate School Education and the 50th Anniversary of Its Establishment</div>
  <div class="proj-meta">Dec. 2023 ~ Feb. 2024 | Environmental Planning Institute, Seoul National University</div>
  <div class="proj-desc">Contributed to producing the commemorative white paper documenting half a century of the graduate school.</div>
</div>

<div class="proj-item">
  <div class="proj-title">A Study Commemorating the 55th Anniversary of Environmental Graduate School Education and the 50th Anniversary of Its Establishment</div>
  <div class="proj-meta">May 2023 ~ Nov. 2023 | Environmental Planning Institute, Seoul National University</div>
  <div class="proj-desc">A commemorative study reflecting on what the graduate school has taught and researched over fifty years.</div>
</div>

<div class="proj-item">
  <div class="proj-title">Strategies to Improve Social Acceptance through Environmental and Safety Responses for a 100MW-Class Floating Solar Power Plant</div>
  <div class="proj-meta">Jun. 2020 ~ Feb. 2021 | Korea Institute of Energy Technology Evaluation and Planning (Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy)</div>
  <div class="proj-desc">Researched ways to improve social acceptance of large-scale (100MW-class) floating solar power by addressing environmental and safety concerns.</div>
</div>
