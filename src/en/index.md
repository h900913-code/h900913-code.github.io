## About

Park Jeonghyeon is a Ph.D. candidate in Environmental Planning at the Graduate School of Environmental Studies, Seoul National University. His research focuses on Environmental Communications and Text Mining, exploring topics such as climate crisis discourse analysis, energy policy, and inter-Korean environmental research cooperation.

He earned a Bachelor's degree in Political Science and Diplomacy with a double major in Sociology from Korea University, and a Master's degree in Environmental Planning from the Graduate School of Environmental Studies, Seoul National University.
