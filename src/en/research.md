<div class="page-intro">
  <p class="eyebrow">Research Agenda</p>
  <h1>Research Interests</h1>
  <p>His work connects environmental communication and text mining across climate crisis discourse, energy policy, inter-Korean environmental cooperation, and the analysis of IPCC assessment reports.</p>
</div>

<div class="feature-grid">
  <section class="feature-card">
    <p class="feature-index">01</p>
    <h2>Environmental Communication</h2>
    <p>He studies how climate crisis and environmental issues are framed and circulated in media and the public sphere. By analyzing online articles and comments, he examines the discourse structure of climate-related communication.</p>
  </section>

  <section class="feature-card">
    <p class="feature-index">02</p>
    <h2>Text Mining</h2>
    <p>He uses topic modeling, network analysis, and other computational text analysis methods to extract meaningful themes and patterns from large-scale textual data in environmental and energy research.</p>
  </section>
</div>
