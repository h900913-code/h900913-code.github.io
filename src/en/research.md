# Research Interests

## Environmental Communications

Research on how climate crisis and environmental issues are covered and communicated in media and the public sphere. Analyzes internet articles and comments to understand the discourse structure of the climate crisis and explores the impact of media on environmental awareness.

## Text Mining

Utilizes computer-based text analysis methodologies such as topic modeling and network analysis to extract meaningful patterns and themes from large-scale text data in the environmental and energy fields. Analyzes diverse text sources including academic papers, news articles, and policy documents.

## Related Research Topics

- Climate crisis discourse analysis
- Energy policy and social acceptance
- Inter-Korean climate change and renewable energy research cooperation
- IPCC Assessment Report analysis

## Research Methods

- Topic Modeling
- Network Analysis
- Content Analysis
- Text mining using R, Python, SQL
- Graph data analysis using Netminer
